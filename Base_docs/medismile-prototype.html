Common questions

Everything you need to know

Can't find your answer? Call us or send a message on WhatsApp.

Do you accept dental insurance?
+
Yes — we accept Humana, Aetna, Delta Dental, Ameritas, UCD, and several other major plans. We also offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance. Call us or send a WhatsApp to verify your coverage before your first visit.
Is Dr. Nelson available at both locations?
+
Yes. Dr. Nelson Marques rotates between our Winter Springs and Ocoee locations. When scheduling, you can request a specific location, and our team will confirm his availability there.
Do you offer emergency dental appointments?
+
Absolutely. We reserve time in our daily schedule for dental emergencies. If you're experiencing a toothache, broken tooth, or lost filling, contact us by phone or WhatsApp and we'll get you seen as soon as possible — often the same day.
Do you provide care in Portuguese?
+
Yes! Dr. Nelson Marques is fully bilingual in English and Portuguese. We proudly serve Orlando's Brazilian community and can communicate with you entirely in your preferred language — from consultation to aftercare.
What should I bring to my first appointment?
+
Please bring a valid photo ID, your dental insurance card (if applicable), a list of any medications you're currently taking, and your completed new patient forms. You can download the forms from our website in advance to save time at check-in.
